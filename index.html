Borreliose:
  • is a infection caused by bacteria infected ticks
  • about 30 - 50% of all ticks are infected
  • Estimated at 1'000 - 2'000 cases per Year
  • Corona Summer: 2020: nearly 3'000
  • Remember that switzerland only has 8 M inhabitatnts
  • No vaccination available
  • entire switzerland is considered risk high risk area
TBE (de: FSME):
  • Viral infection of ticks
  • Much rarer: 20 - 50 per year. Corona year: >100
  • Vaccination available
